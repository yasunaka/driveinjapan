4 STEPS to buy cars
STEP 1

Register

  • First you rigister to JPC TRADE CO. LTD..
  • Please choose Drive in Japan as type.
  • We will respond within short time.
  • If you need any assistance regarding a vehicle or the buying process, please feel free to contact our office.
STEP 2

Searching car

  • Pay advance deposit (100% Refundable) to enable you participate in the auctions.
  • You can search by entering the details (brand, colour, options, etc.) of the car you are looking for in the auction system.
STEP 3

Bid

  • Once you get all the necessary information regarding the vehicle and satisfied, you will set the bidding price.
  • We will start bidding for the vehicle until it reaches your bidding price.
STEP 4

Payment

  • After making a successful bid, Drive in Japan mwill make a payment for the vehicle, and send you the invoice of your balance.
  • After we receive your payment, we will deliver the vehicle to you or arrange for you to pick it up.
